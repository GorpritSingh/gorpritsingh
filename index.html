Design and Manufacturing of Engineered Equipments

In our company, fabrication work plays a crucial role in producing high-quality components. We utilize advanced techniques like cutting, welding, and machining to shape raw materials, ensuring precision and durability in every product. Our skilled team is dedicated to delivering custom solutions that meet the specific needs of various industries, maintaining high standards in both design and execution.

Channel Partners of WEICON GmbH & Co. KG

WEICON GmbH & Co. KG, established in 1947 with headquarters in Münster, operates internationally. We are proud to be a Channel partner of WEICON, offering a comprehensive range of high-quality industrial solutions tailored to various industries. WEICON’s product lineup includes Adhesives and sealants, such as cyanoacrylate adhesives for fast bonding, epoxy adhesives for strong and durable connections, MS polymer adhesives for flexible and weather-resistant bonding, and silicone sealants for high-temperature and elastic sealing applications. Additionally, WEICON provides Assembly pastes and greases, including anti-seize pastes to prevent corrosion and high-temperature greases for extreme conditions. Their Technical sprays and liquids cater to maintenance needs with rust protection sprays, industrial cleaners, and welding protection sprays. For Surface and corrosion protection, WEICON offers zinc sprays for cold galvanization and specialized coatings to safeguard metal surfaces from rust and environmental damage. Moreover, WEICON TOOLS delivers professional-grade stripping and cutting tools, including cable strippers and coaxial cable tools, ensuring precision and efficiency in electrical and mechanical work. As a trusted channel partner, we are committed to supplying these innovative and high-performance products to industries such as manufacturing, automotive, shipbuilding, and electrical engineering, providing reliable solutions for maintenance, repair, and assembly processes.
