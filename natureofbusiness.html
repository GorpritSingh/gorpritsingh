UNOAM CO SACHMAN SAFETY SYSTEM LLP

UNOAM CO SACHMAN SAFETY SYSTEM LLP specializes in a wide range of industrial safety services and solutions, including:

  • Valves Overhauling: We specialize in the overhauling of valves, providing comprehensive inspection, repair, and testing services to ensure optimal performance and reliability. Our expertise covers a wide range of industrial valves, restoring them to peak efficiency while meeting industry standards. With advanced tools and precise methodologies, we help extend the lifespan of valves, minimize downtime, and enhance operational safety.
  • Manufacturing Test Bench: We specialize in the manufacturing of test benches designed for precise testing and calibration of industrial valves. Our test benches are built for durability, accuracy, and efficiency, ensuring reliable performance under various pressure and operational conditions. Designed to meet industry standards, our solutions help streamline valve testing, maintenance, and overhauling processes for enhanced safety and productivity.
  • Safety Valve Service: We provide specialized safety valve services, including inspection, pressure testing, set-point calibration, and complete refurbishment to ensure compliance with industry standards such as ASME, API, and ISO. Our process includes hydrostatic and pneumatic testing, lapping and reconditioning of sealing surfaces, spring tension adjustments, and leak detection to maintain precise operational integrity. Utilizing advanced test benches and certified procedures, we ensure that safety valves function reliably under designated pressure conditions, preventing system overpressure and ensuring operational safety.

UNOAM FLOW CONTROL LLP

UNOAM FLOW CONTROL LLP specializes in providing high-quality flow control products, including various types of butterfly valves for industrial applications.

  • Concentric Butterfly Valve: The concentric butterfly valve has the disc, stem, and seat all aligned concentrically, meaning they are aligned on a single axis.
  • Eccentric Butterfly Valve: Used in industries such as chemical, oil and gas, power generation, and steam systems, where more robust sealing is required.
  • Tricentric Butterfly Valve: Common in power generation, steam systems, petrochemical, and critical industrial processes that require tight shut-off and reliability.
